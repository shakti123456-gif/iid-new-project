ABOUT US

Our Company

Indian Institute of Drones, with Government, registered Regn No. 4/1267/159/206/67 provides the nationâ€™s highest quality drone training with a simple yet comprehensive model for enterprise clients, government agencies, public safety departments, and individuals.

Our highly trained staff has many years of experience in the aviation industry and also in the drone industry, primarily from the defence background with certifications from the Ministry of Defence through their respective wings (Army, Navy or Air Force). This experience is now being extended to the rapidly growing UAV (Drone) market in the civilian sector. So our students will be certified by Ex- Military UAV pilot. We also periodically affiliate with various organisations around the world for creating a better career for our students.

It is our intention to offer the required skill set that tick the DGCA boxes as well as delivering courses that are insightful, fun and above all extend the superior safety and security record that the Indian Aviation Industry enjoys.

Indian Institute of Drones is a Training wing of Johnnette Technologies Pvt Ltd which is present in three different locations at Noida/Chennai/Podili which has very good connectivity. Podili location conducts primarily fixed-wing training and is also our Flight Test Centre. The training at the institute will be carried out by highly qualified Instructors having more than 1500 hours of combined flying experience, giving the learners an in-depth knowledge in unmanned flying and in unmanned systems technologies as per the DGCA CAR.

Vision

Indian Institute of Drones strives to enhance the future of the youth with unprecedented standards in interactive teaching and practical training in the field of unmanned systems technology. We will continue on our endeavours to make a better world.

Mission

  • To impart excellence in Drone technology.
  • To produce the most competent drone pilots.
  • To become the best Drone training institute in the world.
  • To revolutionize the future of drone industry.
  • To ingrain the highest flight safety standards in the Drone community.
  • To teach how to protect life, property, nature and self with Drone technology.
